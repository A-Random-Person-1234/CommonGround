CommonGround

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 8 July 2026

1. Introduction

CommonGround is a shared calendar room app that helps people compare availability and plan group events. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and how Google Calendar data is handled when a user chooses to connect Google.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect or store:

  • Room names and room codes.
  • Display names.
  • Participant colours and room participation settings.
  • Group event proposals, including titles, times, locations, descriptions, invitees, and related settings.
  • Event votes, such as Yes, Maybe, and No.
  • Event comments.
  • Email address and profile information if you connect Google.
  • Google Calendar free/busy availability data.
  • Session and cookie data needed to keep you signed in, keep you in rooms, and remember room membership.

3. Google Calendar Data

CommonGround uses Google Calendar access only after you choose to connect Google. The app reads free/busy availability blocks so room members can compare when people are available.

CommonGround does not display private Google Calendar event titles, descriptions, locations, or attendees to other room members by default. Other room members can see that you are busy at a time, but they cannot see the private details of the Google Calendar event behind that busy block.

If you later choose to send Google Calendar invites or sync CommonGround events back to Google Calendar, the app may request additional permission to create calendar events on your behalf.

4. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Show shared availability in rooms.
  • Show participants, display names, colours, and connection status.
  • Create and display group event proposals.
  • Let participants vote, comment, and respond to event proposals.
  • Maintain sessions and room membership.
  • Sync, refresh, or update calendar availability.
  • Support optional Google Calendar event creation if you enable that feature.

5. How Information Is Shared

Room participants can see room-level information, including display names, busy/free blocks, group event proposals, votes, comments, and other details that are part of the shared room experience.

Private Google Calendar event titles, descriptions, locations, and attendees are not shown to other room members by default. We do not sell your data. Information may be shared only as needed to operate the service, comply with law, protect rights and safety, or with your consent.

6. Google API Limited Use

CommonGround's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

7. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored so the app can operate rooms, maintain sessions, and support calendar sync. We use reasonable security measures designed to protect user information. No internet service can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

OAuth tokens, if stored, are used only to maintain calendar connection and support the calendar features you have enabled. They should be protected and not shared with other users.

8. Data Retention

Room data may remain until the room is deleted, the service removes it, or deletion is otherwise requested where supported. You can revoke Google access by disconnecting the app where available or through your Google account permissions.

9. User Choices

You can choose not to connect Google Calendar. If guest mode is available, you may use the app as a guest. You can disconnect Google Calendar, leave rooms where supported, delete room content where supported, or request deletion of data by contacting us.

10. Children

The service is not intended for children below the relevant legal age in their location. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review and remove it where appropriate.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When it changes, the effective date above will be updated.
